MovieMachine Movies TV Shows Music

Overview: Since we often struggle with finding a good show to binge, movie to watch, or music to listen to, we want to make a website that decides for us. We want to enter interests and other TV shows/movies/music that we like and obtain a list of recommendations that fit our taste.

Specifications: For the TV shows, music and the movies, there will be a type of form with drop downs and image selection that users will fill out. Once they click submit, the website will create a table with recommended titles listed, their ratings, and a link to reviews of them.

Course Relevance: This website will have homepage and overview files as well as separate tabs for TV show recommendations, music recommendations and movie recommendations.

Advanced Feature: This website includes complex backgrounds using the parallax feature in order to have the images scroll with the page’s movement.
