Home Movies TV Shows

Overview: Since we often struggle with finding a good show to binge, movie to watch, we want to make a website that decides for us. We want to enter interests and other TV shows/movies that we like and obtain a list of recommendations that fit our taste.

Specifications: For the TV shows and the movies, there will be a type of form that users can fill out. Once they click submit, the website will display a genre with 3 images boxes containing recommended titles and their ratings.

Course Relevance: This website will have homepage and overview files as well as separate tabs for TV show recommendations and movie recommendations.

Advanced Feature: This website includes complex backgrounds using the parallax feature in order to have the images scroll with the page’s movement. Additionally, we will include a noise when a button is clicked.
